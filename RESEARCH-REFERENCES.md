### Typography
- **72%** use serif fonts for headlines (Playfair, Instrument Serif, Times)
- **85%** use system fonts or Inter for body text
- **Average headline size:** 80-120px on desktop
- **Trend:** Uppercase tracking for labels/tags

### Color
- **60%** use dark theme (black/very dark gray)
- **30%** use light minimal theme
- **10%** use colorful/gradient backgrounds
- **Accent colors:** Neon greens, purples, oranges

### Layout
- **Hero:** 100vh or larger
- **Projects:** Large thumbnails (not small grid)
- **Whitespace:** Generous (80-120px between sections)
- **Navigation:** Hidden or minimal

### Animation
- **Scroll-triggered reveals** - 90% of sites
- **Hover effects** - 100% of sites
- **Page transitions** - 40% of sites
- **Custom cursor** - 25% of sites
